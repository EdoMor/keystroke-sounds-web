New in 1.3

More than sound — insight, and control

Keystroke Sounds now understands how you type and respects when you need silence — two upgrades that make it a tool you keep, not a toy you mute.

Insight

Typing stats & a live key heatmap

A new Stats tab turns your typing into a picture: watch your words-per-minute climb in real time, and see the whole keyboard light up cool → hot by how often you hit each key.

  • Live WPM plus session, daily and all-time totals.
  • Colour heatmap of every key — hover for its exact count.
  • 100% private. Counts stay on your PC and reset any time.

Control

Quiet hours & a global mute hotkey

Keep the clacks for when you want them. Schedule automatic silence, or mute instantly from anywhere — without ever opening the window.

  • Quiet hours: set a daily window (even past midnight) and it mutes itself.
  • Global hotkey: one shortcut (default Ctrl+Alt+M) toggles sound system-wide.
  • Perfect for calls and screen-shares — your stats keep counting while muted.

Why you'll love it

🔊 A sound for every key

Letters spread across a sample pool, with distinct sounds for Space, Enter, Backspace, Tab and modifiers — so typing actually sounds good, not like one key on repeat.

🛟 Lives in the tray

Close or minimise and it tucks into the Windows taskbar arrow, playing quietly in the background across every app.

🎨 Make it yours

Build your own packs from any audio, swap sounds per key, or load a set from a link — your custom packs survive updates.

📈 Know your keyboard

Live WPM, daily & all-time counts and a colour key heatmap — all tracked privately on your own machine.

🌙 Quiet when you need it

Quiet-hours scheduling and a global mute hotkey keep things silent during calls, focus time or late nights.

♻️ Stays fresh

One-click updates, plus a growing library of downloadable sound packs right inside the app.
